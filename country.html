Documents

  • 1 completed Visa Application Form signed by traveller Visa Application form or Online form

  • 1 original Photograph, Size 48mm x 33mm (Scans will not be accepted). Requirements for photography

  • Original passport with at least 6 months of remaining validity and with 2 blank visa pages, a photocopy of the passport's data page and the photo page if it is separate. Requirements for passport.

  • Photocopy of previous Chinese passports or previous Chinese visas (applicable for those who were Chinese citizens and have obtained foreign citizenship). If you are applying for a Chinese visa for the first time, you should provide the last Chinese passport held and a photocopy of its data page.

  • An original confirmation of the payment of the travel expenses by the employer
