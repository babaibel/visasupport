Business Invitation for the Russian Federation

Additionally to your visa application you need an invitation of your Russian business partner for all business travel to the Russian Federation. Depending on the Federal state the responsible consulates in charge define their own requirements. Furthermore there are special agreements for travelers coming from countries of the European Union.

Visa Support & Business Solutions GmbH has specialized on the procurement of such invitations. Due to our cooperation partners in the Russian Federation as well as our long-lasting experience with different European consulates our team is well accustomed with all requirements.

Especially getting these necessary invitations can easily result in delays that may jeopardize the success of your trip.

Tourist Reference for the Russian Federation

For a tourist trip to the Russian Federation you need a hotel confirmation, called ‘hotel voucher’. Surcharges apply if your hotel issues the voucher. Alternatively we can arrange the confirmation through our authorized travel agencies.

Obviously the procurement via Visa Support is more reliable; your German speaking contact person in Germany is always available for you!

Business Invitation for Kazakhstan

As for the Russian Federation also for certain business visa for travel to Kazakhstan an invitation of the business partner is mandatory.

Our team is familiar with the requirements of the consulates and can help you purposefully to realize the essential documents for your visa application.
